About

Engineer by training,
Leader by experience

Graduating from Korea Maritime & Ocean University in 2010 with a Bachelor's in Marine System Engineering, I began my career at sea as a Marine Engineer with Hanjin Shipping — operating container vessels up to 13,100 TEU across the globe.

From the engine room to the boardroom, my career has evolved through marine engine technical service at NSCA Engine Service & Hyundai Global Service, vessel management and project engineering at Crowley Maritime, and now leading marine electrification projects for marine customers at Caterpillar.

Bilingual in English and Korean, I bridge technical expertise with cross-cultural communication — serving as a liaison between engineering teams and customers worldwide.
